FELLOWSHIP

WHAT WE DO?
Fuelbee offers a holistic fellowship program for developing young talents in our neighbourhood, providing a structured learning experience to harness their talents and and channelize their energy. The fellowship is designed such that young fellows gets trained, oriented and guided to improve their skills, find their passion and enhance their knowledge. For certain deserving and selective candidates, the program is also offered as paid fellowship.

KEY OBJECTIVES

  • To develop young minds to actively participate in bridging the educational gaps prevalent in the society through fellowships.
  • Passionately support FuelBee’s existing educational and social initiatives by partaking in its training to develop fellow’s skills and abilities.
  • To nurture young fellows to become leader material for their future career and personal advancement along with a bend for social development.
  • Creating a network of young minds with similar social interest to positively impact the educational needs in the society.

FUELBEE FELLOWSHIP

The ideal FuelBee Fellow is someone who has a deep passion towards teaching children and contribute to educational ecosystem, continuously seeks for knowledge, disciplined and wants to be a role model for children, questions diligently and having a deep sense of commitment and passion towards work
